About We

NewCo Foundation is a private 501 (c)(3) non-profit driven by the incubation of ideas, products, services, and companies founded by or employing students.

Our Work

We operate on and off campus programs that provide employment opportunities for high school and college students to make meaningful contributions to startup companies.

Our Impact

We accelerate readiness for college and careers by means of an entrepreneurial apprenticeship model. We provide student internships, earned scholarships, school equipment and supplies.
