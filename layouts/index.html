About Us

NewCo Foundation is a 501 (c)(3) non-profit driven by the incubation of ideas, products, services, and companies founded by or employing students.

Our Work

We support on and off campus programs that provide employment opportunities for high school and college students to make meaningful contributions to startup endeavours. We poperate Bask-Office Support Services for School-Based Enterprise (BOSS for SBE) to enable and streamline on-campus businesses in career education programs.

Our Impact

We accelerate readiness for college and careers by means of an entrepreneurial apprenticeship model. We provide student internships, earned scholarships, faculty stipends, school equipment and supplies.
