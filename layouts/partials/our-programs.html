Supported Programs

RCMakes is a school-based, student-operated non-profit corporation at Rancho Campana High School. RCMakes produces goods and services, provides internships and student employment, awards earned academic scholarships, and incubates startup businesses.

RCMakes operates a community makerspace and job shop, conducts after-school and summer learning workshops, provides coding mentors to instructors, produces professional videos, does software development and hosts special events.
